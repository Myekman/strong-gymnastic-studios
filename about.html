Our Story

A gymnastics association since 1990, placed in Stockholm!
In the beginning the club was small. the offer was limited to artistic gymnastics for girls 8-12 years old, only with 2 coaches. Malin Larsson and Emma Lind, who also are the founders of the association. Today after over 30 years we have grown at a rapid pace. We are now proud to offer every type there is of different kind of gymnastics. Both artistic, rhythmic and team gymnastics. We have over 20 coaches who are committed to the club.
