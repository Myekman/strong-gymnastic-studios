Strong Gymnastic Studios

Mens Gymnastic

Men's gymnastics is available in squad gymnastics and artistic gymnastics.
Artistic gymnastics is practiced alone with bars and rings, while group gymnastics competes together with carpet and treads.

See below for training times.

Womans Gymnastic

Women's gymnastics is available in group, artistic or rhythmic gymnastics.
In artistic gymnastics, bars and rings are involved. In rhythmic we learn the branches, bands and dance.

See below for training times.

Kids / Yought Gymnastic

Gymnastics is available for different ages, kids 6-9 and yought 10-15. You can choose between styles:
Group, Artistic or Rhythmic Gymnastics. In artistic gymnastics, bars and rings are involved. In rhythmic we learn the branches, bands and dance. We have different ages in our groups.

see below for training times.
